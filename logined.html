About VAS system

VAS system

This system is name as the SAV which stand for the Scenes And Voting system for Wesley College. The SAV include many popular scenes of Wesley college such as “the road of pearl”, “the pearl”,” the pearl wall”, “library” ,”self-study room” , “the heavenly lounge”, “ the act room” , “the math room” , “the design and technology rom” , “campus TV” and the “MMLC” multimedia learning center. As such, the voting system contains various choices for student to vote for their most attractive scene. Moreover, the system allow user to add their most attractive scene after the Approval of the admin.If the scene is not include in the voting system before. This enhances the content of the voting system and let users to exchange their favorite scenes through the voting system and enrich the content of the voting system. Also the voting system will show the real time voting result on the website to let user to know the latest result of the pull. Furthermore the SAV will request user to provide their email which let the user to confirm their vote has been accept or not. In addition, the system will provide the data analysis function in the website providing the real time pie chart for user. Last but not least, the SAV will provide the message board for users to let them to left their message in the webpage. This improves the interactive between the user and the website and between user and user.
